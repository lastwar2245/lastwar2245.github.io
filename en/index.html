🧠 General principles

  • Prepare actions in advance, then claim them on the correct day.
  • Keep radars and gathering for the days when they score points.
  • Keep construction, research, heroes, training and healing for their matching VS theme.
  • Keep building rewards and red dots whenever possible.
  • Make alliance donations EVERY DAY to speed up collective bonuses.
  • Heal in small batches, around 50 troops, during battles.
  • Build a strong T1 before spreading resources across T2/T3.

🪵 Lumberjack tips and tricks

Practical rules to progress faster, score better in VS, and avoid costly mistakes.

⏳ Stocker 🎯 Claim on the right day 🤝 Play as an alliance 🛡️ Protect yourself 📈 Prioritize T1

⏳ 1. Store before clicking

  • Do not click too quickly on red dots .
  • Complete some actions the day before, then claim them the next day if the VS theme matches.
  • Keep rewards that can wait: buildings, research, chests, shards, tickets.

🤝 2. Make alliance donations — EVERY DAY

  • Make donations EVERY DAY to unlock collective bonuses.
  • Prioritize technologies useful for VS, power and combat.
  • An alliance that donates progresses faster and scores better.

🔍 3. Radars: prepare the day before

  • Les radars sont importants les jours où Complete 1 Radar task scores points.
  • Prepare mainly Sunday evening for Monday, Tuesday evening for Wednesday and Thursday evening for Friday.
  • Watch the maximum radar-task limit.

🌾 4. Monday gathering

  • Start gathering on Sunday evening.
  • Les faire terminer after 04:00 .
  • Best: claim after 04:05 to avoid scoring errors.

🎁 5. Building gifts and rewards

  • Do not automatically open building-related gifts.
  • Keep them for Tuesday or Friday if it scores VS points.
  • Same logic for research, drone components, heroes and training.

🪖 6. Soldiers: staggered production

  • Restart barracks from Sunday to rebuild after VS / Capitol.
  • Produce all week without burning speedups.
  • Make the big push on Friday: training + promotions N5 → N6 → N7 .

🦸 7. Heroes: upgrade by 10-level steps

  • Do not upgrade heroes randomly.
  • Viser les paliers utiles : 10 / 20 / 30 / 40 .
  • Priority to the main team and heroes used every day.

🧬 8. Teams: T1 first

  • One strong T1 is better than three average teams.
  • Defenders in front, attackers and supports in back.
  • Gear: armure/radar on frontline, canon/puce on backline.

🛡️ 9. Shield before Enemy Buster

  • Friday night: shield before sleeping.
  • Cover at least until after 04h00 / 04h05 .
  • For absent players: shield until Saturday evening.

🏥 10. Heal in small batches

  • Heal in batches of around 50 troops .
  • Ask alliance help, restart, repeat.
  • Avoid clogging the hospital during battles.

🧱 11. If you forget a shield

  • Remove teams from the defense wall.
  • Goal: limit troop losses.
  • The enemy scores less, and the base becomes less profitable to attack.

🕵️ 12. Stealth attack from mine to mine

  • Approach discreetly by moving from mine to mine or nearby point.
  • Strike at the last moment on a profitable target.
  • Return under shield or to the hive immediately afterwards.

🕵️ 13. Scouting & frenzy bonus

  • Before an attack, scout a mine or another base to trigger / benefit from the frenzy .
  • This logic can also help during Marshal exercises or similar events.
  • Then: strike quickly, then shield again as soon as possible.

🚫 14. Server 2245 rule

  • On 2245: plunder nothing.
  • No trucks, tasks, gathering, bases or resources.
  • Targets are VS opponents or enemy servers.

🏠 15. Return to the hive

  • After an attack, do not stay exposed.
  • Use the free VS TPs to return cleanly.
  • Shield again after the action as soon as possible, ideally at the end of the attack frenzy .

📢 16. Alliance communication

  • Warn before attacks.
  • Share your position on the opposing server.
  • Coordinate attackers, garrisons, scouting and absent players.

🧠 17. Final lumberjack rule

  • Prepare the day before.
  • Claim on the right day.
  • Do not waste.
  • Do not give away free points.

🤝🎁 Alliance donations & reward storage

🤝 Alliance donations — EVERY DAY

  • Make donations EVERY DAY .
  • Prioritize technologies useful for VS and alliance power.
  • Donations provide collective benefits and make progression easier.

🎁 Rewards / gifts

  • Do not systematically open building gifts.

On Saturday, it is possible to play more discreetly by moving a team from mine to mine or a nearby point, then hit the enemy at the last moment. The goal is to reduce exposure time, surprise the target and limit retaliation.

  • Only strike a profitable and coordinated target.

🧬 Strategy guide — Team optimization

Strong T1 first → decent T2 next → T3 later.

🛡️ Frontline

  • 2 heroes in front.
  • Defenders / tanks.
  • Role: absorb damage.
  • Gear: armor + radar.

🔥 Backline

  • 3 heroes in back.
  • Attaquants / supports.
  • Role: damage and bonuses.
  • Gear: cannon + chip.

🧩 Type bonus

Composition Bonus Recommandation
5 heroes of the same type +20 % Long-term goal
4 + 1 +15 % Very good compromise
3 + 2 +10 % Good mixed team
3 + 1 + 1 +5 % Temporaire
2 + 2 + 1 Weak Avoid if possible
